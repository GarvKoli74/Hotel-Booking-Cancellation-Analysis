# Hotel Booking Cancellation Analysis (Excel)

## 📌 Business Problem
A major hotel chain has been experiencing volatile, high cancellation rates. This unpredictability has led to inefficient staffing, poor inventory management, and millions in uncaptured revenue. The objective of this project was to analyze historical booking data to isolate the core drivers of cancellations and provide actionable operational recommendations.

## 🛠️ Data Stack & Methodology
* **Data Volume:** 1 Lakh+ historical customer records.
* **Data Cleaning & Engineering:** Handled missing values, standardized country codes, and created calculated fields for booking lead times.
* **Analysis Techniques:** Advanced Pivot Tables (utilized XLOOKUP and VLOOKUP for data mapping), conditional aggregation, and trend analysis across multiple dimensions.
* **Interactive Dashboarding:** Engineered a dynamic executive dashboard equipped with custom multi-select Slicers (Arrival Year, Guest Type, Location, Month) to allow stakeholders to perform self-service exploratory analysis.

## 📈 Executive Insights & Business Impact
* **Lead Time Risk Factor:** Bookings made more than 60 days in advance showed a 40% higher probability of cancellation. Recommended implementing automated email/SMS confirmations at the 45-day mark.
* **Market Segment Vulnerability:** Transient customer segments accounted for the highest volume of cancellations. Suggested dynamic pricing rules or non-refundable deposit requirements during peak operational months.
